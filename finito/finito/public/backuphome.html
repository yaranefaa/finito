How Finito can help you!

Scheduling your tasks is a chore! We are here because it shouldn't be...

Using several psychological theories of productivity, Finito has found a way to make those hard-to-do tasks less hard!

Productivity Training

Finito helps ensure you build your way up to productivity gradually enough to avoid feeling of burn-out and tiredness.

Resistance

By indicating your level of resistance for a task finito can suggest an optimal date & time for your desired task to make sure your schedule is easy to stick to!

You get the final say

While it's easy to tell you what to do, sometimes you want things done your way! And this why finito aims to not constraint users to our formula and allow for personalitzation and freedom of choice.

3 SCHEDULE MODES
